Orchard Ridge Dress Code
The following guidelines are provided by Orchard Ridge in regards to attire:
Men may wear business casual attire with neatly pressed fitted slacks and appropriate shirt with collar. Ties are optional.
Woman may wear dresses, skirts, business suits, pant suits and dress slacks with appropriate tops
Distressed denim jeans and t-shirts are considered inappropriate club attire.
